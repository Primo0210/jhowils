About Me

Where Light Meets Emotion
Through the Lens

Hi, I’m Jhowils but you can call me Primo, with a deep love for cinematography as both an art and a hobby, passionate about cinematic portraits and street photography. I’m drawn to the contrast of neon lights, urban textures, and the raw emotion found in unfiltered moments. My work is all about capturing stories as they naturally unfold, blending atmosphere with authenticity.

For me, every shoot is a collaboration. I work with both natural and artificial light to create images that feel alive moody, vibrant, and uniquely yours. Whether it’s during golden hour or in the middle of a city night, I aim to bring intention, emotion, and a cinematic touch to every frame.
